Frequently Asked Questions

Common Photography Questions

What camera for travel?

Look for a lightweight mirrorless body. Fujifilm X-T50, Sony A6700, or Canon R50 are great options.

How many megapixels do I need?

24MP is plenty for most uses. 45MP+ is for professional studio work.

Is full-frame better than APS-C?

Full-frame gives better low-light performance. APS-C is lighter and cheaper.

How long do camera batteries last?

DSLR: 800-1200 shots. Mirrorless: 300-600 shots. Always carry a spare.
