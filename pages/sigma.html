About the Brand

The Art of Lens Making

Sigma ek Japanese brand hai jo apni innovative lens technology aur exceptional optical quality ke liye duniya bhar mein mashhoor hai. Sigma ne yeh sabit kiya ke high-quality lenses affordable bhi ho sakte hain.

Sigma ke Art, Sports, aur Contemporary series lenses professional photographers ko unmatched sharpness, beautiful bokeh, aur stunning color rendering provide karte hain. Har lens Japan mein haath se banaya jaata hai aur 100% quality check ke baad release kiya jaata hai.

Art Series Lenses Sports Series Contemporary Series Foveon Sensor Technology Japanese Craftsmanship Global Vision

Brand History

1961
Sigma Corporation Japan mein establish hui. Shuruaat mein lens manufacturing aur camera accessories banaye.
2002
Sigma SD9 — pehla camera jisne Foveon X3 sensor ko use kiya. Unique technology jo RGB colors ko alag-alag layers mein capture karti hai.
2012
Sigma Art Series launch — professional lenses jinhone industry mein naya standard set kiya. 50mm f/1.4 Art ne sab ko hairan kar diya.
2016
Sigma Sports Series launch — wildlife aur sports photographers ke liye professional telephoto lenses.
2018
Sigma Contemporary Series launch — compact aur lightweight lenses jo everyday photography ke liye perfect hain.
Present
Sigma ab Canon RF, Nikon Z, Sony E, L-Mount, aur Fujifilm X mount ke liye wide range of native lenses provide kar raha hai.
