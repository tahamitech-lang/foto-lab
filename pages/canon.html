About the Brand

More than a Camera Company

Canon ki shuruaat 1937 mein Tokyo mein Precision Optical Instruments Laboratory ke naam se hui. Inka ek hi mission tha — Japan ki sabse behtareen camera banana. Aur wo mission aaj tak qaim hai.

Aaj Canon imaging, office solutions, printing aur medical technology mein global leader hai — lekin dil se yeh camera company hai jo light, glass aur colour ke quality ke liye janab janti hai.

Japanese Precision In-house Lens Manufacturing Dual Pixel AF Technology RF & EF Mount Systems L-Series Glass World-class Service Network

Brand Timeline

1937
Canon founded in Tokyo. First camera — the Hansa Canon — released, rivaling German optics.
1959
Canon Canonflex launched — Japan's first 35mm SLR camera, redefining professional photography.
1987
EF lens mount introduced — the revolutionary autofocus system still used in Canon DSLRs today.
2003
Canon EOS 300D — the world's first consumer DSLR under $1000. Digital photography for everyone.
2018
EOS R system launched. New RF mount ushers Canon into the mirrorless era with groundbreaking speed.
2024
Canon EOS R5 Mark II released — 45MP, 8K RAW video, AI-powered subject tracking. The benchmark.

Why Choose Canon

Canon ki proprietary Dual Pixel CMOS AF har lighting condition mein sabse tez aur accurate autofocus deti hai — even -6 EV darkness mein.

L-Series Glass

Canon ki legendary L-Series lenses — jinki pehchan red ring hai — in-house fluorite aur UD elements ke sath manufactured hain for unmatched clarity.

Cinema Grade Video

4K 60fps se 8K RAW tak, Canon cameras Netflix-approved productions aur broadcast studios worldwide mein use hote hain.

Build Quality

Magnesium alloy bodies with weather sealing rated for dust, moisture, aur sub-zero temperatures. Built to work where you work.
