About the Brand

Timeless Classic Elegance

Yashica ne hamesha un logon ke liye cameras banaye hain jo photography ke asli art aur tactile feel ko pasand karte hain. Unke cameras apni compact size aur shandaar lens quality ke liye mashhoor rahe hain.

Chahe aap street photography ke shaukeen hon ya film look ki nostalgia chahte hon, Yashica ka minimalist approach aapko har click par ek nayi kahani sunane ka mauka deta hai.

Brand History

1949
Yashica ki shuruat Nagano, Japan mein hui. Shuruaat mein lens aur camera parts banaye.
1950s
TLR (Twin Lens Reflex) cameras ke sath duniya bhar mein naam kamaya. Yashica Mat series ne camera lovers ko mutasir kiya.
1960s
Yashica Electro 35 launched — duniya ka pehla compact rangefinder camera jisne auto-exposure technology ko introduce kiya.
1970s
Carl Zeiss ke sath historic partnership, jisne high-quality lenses aur cameras ko janam diya. Contax series ne professional photography ko naya maqam diya.
1980s
Yashica FX-3 Super 2000 — fully mechanical SLR camera jo aaj bhi collectors ki pehli pasand hai.
Present
Yashica aaj bhi vintage lovers ke liye 'Yashica' brand name ke sath nayi digi-film cameras aur accessories ke sath wapsi kar raha hai.

Minimalist Design

Iconic Legacy
