About the Brand

Timeless Classic Elegance

Yashica ne hamesha un logon ke liye cameras banaye hain jo photography ke asli art aur tactile feel ko pasand karte hain. Unke cameras apne compact size aur shandaar lens quality ke liye mashhoor rahe hain.

Chahe aap street photography ke shaukeen hon ya film look ki nostalgia chahte hon, Yashica ka minimalist approach aapko har click par ek nayi kahani sunane ka mauka deta hai.

Iconic Retro Design Tactile Manual Controls Compact Film Aesthetics Legendary Optics

Brand History

1949
Yashica ki shuruat Nagano, Japan mein hui.
1950s
TLR (Twin Lens Reflex) cameras ke sath duniya bhar mein naam kamaya.
1970s
Carl Zeiss ke sath historic partnership, jisne high-quality lenses aur cameras ko janam diya.
Present
Yashica aaj bhi vintage lovers ke liye 'Yashica' brand name ke sath nayi digi-film cameras aur accessories ke sath wapsi kar raha hai.
