About the Brand

Compact & Weather Ready

Olympus ne duniya ko dikhaya ke camera kitna chhota aur lightweight ho sakta hai. Apni Micro Four Thirds technology ke sath, Olympus ne professional quality ko pocket-friendly size mein de diya.

Chahe aap travel photography karein, wildlife shoot karein, ya rainy days mein bhi shooting karna chahein — Olympus ki weather-sealed bodies aur 5-axis IBIS aapko har situation mein behtareen results deti hain.

Brand History

1919
Olympus Optical Co. Ltd. Japan mein establish hui. Pehle microscopes aur optical instruments banaye.
1936
Olympus Semi-Olympus I — pehla camera launch hua. Japan ka pehla compact camera.
1959
Olympus PEN series launched — half-frame camera jisne photography ko accessible banaya.
2008
Olympus aur Panasonic ne Micro Four Thirds system introduce kiya — mirrorless revolution ka aghaaz.
2013
Olympus OM-D E-M1 launched — weather-sealed professional mirrorless camera.
Present
OM System ke naam se naye cameras aur lenses launch ho rahe hain, jo Olympus ki legacy ko aage badha rahe hain.

Why Choose Olympus

5-Axis IBIS

TruePic Color

Olympus ka TruePic processor behtareen colors aur skin tones produce karta hai. Out-of-camera JPEGs breathtaking hain.
