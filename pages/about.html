About FOTOLAB

Crafting Photography Excellence Since 1991

Our Story

FOTOLAB was founded in 1991 with a singular vision: to empower photographers with the most innovative and reliable gear. From a small store in Karachi, we’ve grown into a trusted name across Pakistan, serving professionals, hobbyists, and content creators.

We believe in blending cutting-edge technology with timeless craftsmanship. Whether it's a mirrorless camera, a cinema lens, or a studio lighting kit, every product in our catalog is handpicked for its performance and durability.
