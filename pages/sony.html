About the Brand

The Future of Imaging Tech

Sony ne mirrorless cameras ki shuruat karke poori photography industry ka rukh mod diya. Apne behtareen Exmor sensor aur Real-time Tracking AI ke sath, Sony hamesha technology me sabse aage rehta hai.

Aaj Sony duniya ke behtareen full-frame sensors aur autofocus systems banata hai, jo har kism ke commercial, cinematic, aur vlogging projects ke liye perfect hain.

Brand Timeline

2006
Sony Alpha brand ka aghaaz hua, Minolta lens mount asset ko acquire karne ke baad.
2010
NEX series launched — Sony ka pehla kadam mirrorless technology ki dunya me.
2013
Sony Alpha 7 (A7) release hua — duniya ka sabse pehla commercial Full-Frame Mirrorless camera system.
2017
Sony A9 launched — Stacked CMOS sensor ke sath, jisne blackout-free 20fps burst shooting mumkin banayi.
2021
Sony A1 launched — 50MP sensor aur 8K video ke sath flagship performance ka naya standard set kiya.
2025/2026
Sony A7R V aur A9 III high-speed global shutter tech ke sath markets par raj kar rahe hain.
