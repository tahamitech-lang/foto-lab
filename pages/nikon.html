About the Brand

The Heritage of Perfection

Nikon ne hamesha optical engineering ko apni pehchan banaya hai. Inke cameras apne **legendary weather sealing**, shandar body balance, aur **NIKKOR lenses** ki sharp details ke liye poori duniya me jaane jaate hain.

Nikon ka naya **Z-mount** industry ka sabse wide lens mount hai, jo sensor tak zyada roshni (light) pohnchata hai aur low-light conditions me kamaal ki performance daita hai.

Brand Timeline

1917
Nippon Kogaku K.K. ka aghaaz Tokyo, Japan me optical glasses aur binoculars banane se hua.
1959
Nikon F launched — Jisne iconic F-mount system diya aur SLR cameras ko professionals ka benchmark banaya.
1999
Nikon D1 launched — Duniya ka pehla sacha professional digital SLR (DSLR) jo poori tarah Nikon ne khud design kiya.
2018
Nikon Z6 aur Z7 ke sath naye mirrorless safar aur ultra-wide Z-mount ka shandaar aghaaz.
2021
Flagship Nikon Z9 launched — Bagair mechanical shutter ke duniya ka sabse behtareen professional action camera.
2025/2026
Z8, Z6 III, aur retro-styled Z f cameras apni advanced AI tracking ke sath globally top trends me hain.
