About the Brand

The Pinnacle of Medium Format

Hasselblad ek Swedish brand hai jo medium format cameras ke liye duniya bhar mein jaana jaata hai. Inki shuruaat 1941 mein hui aur aaj yeh luxury photography ka ek aisa name hai jo quality, precision, aur elegance ka symbol hai.

Hasselblad cameras ne moon landing se lekar Hollywood films tak, duniya ki tareekh ke kuch sabse iconic moments ko capture kiya hai. Har Hasselblad camera Sweden mein haath se banaya jaata hai, aur har lens ko perfect colour accuracy ke liye calibrate kiya jaata hai.

Swedish Engineering Medium Format Excellence Natural Color Solution NASA Heritage Handcrafted Quality Iconic Design

Brand History

1941
Victor Hasselblad ne Hasselblad company establish ki, jo aerial photography cameras banane ke liye mashhoor hui.
1957
Hasselblad 500C launch — iconic medium format camera system jo aaj bhi photography ka legend hai.
1969
Apollo 11 mission ke dauran Neil Armstrong ne Hasselblad camera se moon landing ki iconic photos li. History in the making.
2000
Hasselblad H-System launch — professional medium format DSLR system.
2016
Hasselblad X1D launch — duniya ka pehla compact medium format mirrorless camera. Photography ka naya daur.
Present
Hasselblad X2D 100C aur H6D system — 100MP medium format cameras jo professional photography ki hadein redefine kar rahe hain.

Why Choose Hasselblad

Swedish Craftsmanship
