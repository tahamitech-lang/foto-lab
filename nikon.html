About Nikon

Precision Optics. Rugged Performance.

Nikon cameras are engineered for creators who push the boundaries. Since 1917, Nikon has refined optics, autofocus, and imaging to deliver sharp, vibrant results in the field and studio.

NIKKOR Lens Heritage Weather-Sealed Bodies High-Speed Tracking AF 4K Video and 3D Tracking

Key Milestones

1917
Nikon founded in Tokyo as Nippon Kogaku — building precision optical instruments for photographers and the military.
1999
Launch of the D1, Nikon's first digital SLR built for professionals and sports photographers.
2018
Introduction of the Z-mount mirrorless system — wider lens mount, faster glass, and enhanced low-light performance.
2024
Nikon Z8 and Z9 continue Nikon's pursuit of speed, reliability, and professional imaging excellence worldwide.
